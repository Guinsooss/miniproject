About Drones
An unmanned aerial vehicle, commonly known as a drone, is an aircraft without a human pilot aboard. UAVs are a component of an unmanned aircraft system; which include a UAV, a ground-based controller, and a system of communications between the two. But even then, there can be confusion among the various types. Let's break them down into two main categories and two subcategories: Military drones, both armed and unarmed. Civilian drones, both recreational and commercial.
